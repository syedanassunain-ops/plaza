Our Story

Plaza started its journey in 1996 as a trusted catering service, serving the most delicious food for weddings, receptions, and grand celebrations across Bangalore. With decades of experience in bringing flavour and joy to thousands of events, we expanded our vision in 2024 by launching Plaza Party Hall — a spacious, elegant venue paired with our legendary catering to offer a complete all-in-one event solution. Whether it's a grand wedding or an intimate gathering, Plaza is your one-stop destination for memories that last a lifetime.
